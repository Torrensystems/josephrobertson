About Us

Heritage, reliability and value-added seafood manufacturing for modern markets.

Who We Are

Joseph Robertson is a long-established seafood business based in Aberdeen, with roots that go back to 1878. Built on generations of industry knowledge, the business has evolved from traditional seafood trading into a modern value-added seafood manufacturer serving retail and foodservice markets.

Today, Joseph Robertson combines heritage with practical manufacturing capability, supplying dependable products and building long-term relationships with customers who value consistency, service and trust.

Leadership Statement

Our identity and purpose

Custodians of Scotland’s seafood, we deliver trusted quality and innovation, lead net zero progress through responsible sourcing, and grow our people and partnerships to create lasting value.

We are heritage-rooted and future-focused, trusted by retailers and households, relentless about quality, and committed to strengthening the North-East economy and seafood industry for the long term.

What We Do

We produce value-added seafood products designed to meet the needs of modern buyers, brands and foodservice operators. Our focus is on delivering products that balance quality, efficiency and scalability, while supporting customers with a reliable and responsive approach.

Markets We Serve

Joseph Robertson works across both retail and foodservice markets.

Our Approach

Strong partnerships built on communication, flexibility and reliability.
